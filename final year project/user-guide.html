User Guide

How to get the best results from Smart Agro

▶ Quick Start (3 Steps)

  1. Step 1: Go to Home — Click Home in the left sidebar.
  2. Step 2: Upload Image — Drag & drop or click to select a clear photo of the plant leaf.
  3. Step 3: View Result — Click Upload & Analyze. Results show disease name, description, prevention, and pesticides.

📷 Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting
  • Close-up shot of a single leaf
  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, WEBP (max 16MB)
  • Model resizes images to 224×224 for CNN input
